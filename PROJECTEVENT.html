Egypt offers both modern malls like Cairo Festival City Mall and Mall of Egypt and traditional markets like Cairo's famous Khan El Khalili bazaar for unique crafts and souvenirs. Other notable shopping destinations include the Zamalek district in Cairo for boutiques and art galleries, and smaller markets in cities like Aswan and Luxor for local specialties such as alabaster and spices.
